About

Settl

Settl was designed to help you find the best places to live. It is created using a unique algorithm that takes into account dozens of characteristics in 7 different categories including nearby amenities, cost of living, crime rates, education, employment, housing and weather. A total Liveability Score is then calculated out of 140+ cities across World.

Using the Liveability Score, Settl ranks more than 35,000 cities and neighborhoods to showcase the best places to live in the World. The list of the best places to live can be sorted by state or as an overall list across all of the World. These top cities are selected by what matters most: lots of local amenities, low crime rates, inexpensive cost of living, great schools, low unemployment rates, affordable housing and sunny weather. Take a look to see what cities rise above all of the rest.
